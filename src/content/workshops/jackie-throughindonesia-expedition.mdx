# Indonesia Photography Expedition 2027

## Bali • Borneo • Sumba

### An International Collaboration Between Through Indonesia and Jackie Schletter Photography

Experience Indonesia through breathtaking landscapes, extraordinary
wildlife encounters, and authentic cultural experiences in a premium
small-group photography expedition designed for passionate photographers
and visual storytellers.

## International Collaboration

Through Indonesia is proud to collaborate with Jackie Schletter
Photography on what is envisioned as a flagship Indonesia photography
experience for international photographers.

This collaboration combines international workshop leadership with deep
local knowledge, creating access to locations, stories, and experiences
that go beyond traditional photography tours.

## Destinations

### Bali

-   Sunrise cultural portraits in Karangasem.
-   Rice terraces and traditional villages.
-   Drone photography opportunities.
-   Temples and authentic Balinese atmosphere.

### Borneo

-   Orangutans in their natural habitat.
-   Klotok river expeditions.
-   Proboscis monkeys and rainforest wildlife.
-   River life and conservation storytelling.

### Sumba

-   Traditional villages.
-   Horse culture and local communities.
-   Savannah landscapes.
-   Dancing trees and cultural portrait sessions.

## Meet Your Hosts

### Jackie Schletter

Wildlife photographer, educator, and expedition leader based in the
United States.

Jackie is passionate about creating meaningful photographic experiences
that connect photographers with wildlife, landscapes, and cultures
around the world.

After visiting Indonesia, Jackie fell in love with the country's
extraordinary diversity and hopes to share that experience with
photographers from around the world.

### Eko Sumartopo

Indonesian landscape and cultural photographer with more than three
decades of experience exploring the Indonesian archipelago.

Eko specializes in visual storytelling, culture, landscape, and
expedition photography while combining international workshop leadership
with deep local knowledge throughout Indonesia.

## Why We Collaborate
