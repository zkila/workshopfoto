# Overview

Experience the extraordinary beauty of Indonesia through an immersive photography expedition beginning in the spiritual landscapes of Bali and continuing across the volcanic heart of Java. This carefully curated expedition combines culture, temples, volcanoes, waterfalls, forests and authentic local experiences for photographers of all levels.

# Drone Photography

Drone flying is welcomed where regulations permit. Indonesia offers spectacular aerial perspectives over rice terraces, volcanoes, temples and forests.

# Drone Mentoring 

Beginners are welcome. Participants with little or no flying experience will receive personal guidance covering flight safety, take-off & landing, camera settings, composition, intelligent flight modes and local regulations. The goal is for beginners to confidently fly independently under Eko's supervision before the expedition ends.

# Difficulty Level

Moderate. Suitable for participants with average fitness. Includes early departures, uneven terrain, moderate hiking and the Mount Ijen sunrise trek.

# Who is This Expedition For?

Suitable for beginners through advanced photographers, drone pilots, landscape, travel, culture and content creators. No professional experience required.
