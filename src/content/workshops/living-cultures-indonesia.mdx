# Overview

An intimate photography expedition exploring Indonesia's rich cultural diversity—from the sacred traditions of Bali, the ancestral villages of Sumba, to the remarkable maritime life of the Bajau people in Southeast Sulawesi. Designed for photographers who value meaningful encounters, authentic storytelling, and immersive cultural experiences.

# Expedition Philosophy

This journey is not about collecting destinations. It is about meeting people, listening to stories, experiencing traditions, and creating photographs with meaning beyond beautiful landscapes.

- Day 1: Arrival in Bali - Transfer to Karangasem, welcome dinner.
- Day 2: Karangasem sunrise, village life, temples, market, portraits, cultural activities.
- Day 3: Pulagan sunrise, flight to Sumba, traditional village at sunset.
- Day 4: Traditional villages, weaving, horse culture, portraits, savanna sunset.
- Day 5: Traditional market, megalithic tombs, waterfall, cultural immersion.
- Day 6: Flight to Buton (via Makassar), sunset at Baubau harbour.
- Day 7: Bajau village, fishermen, environmental portraits, blue hour.
- Day 8: Bajau daily life, boats, women, children, drone photography.
- Day 9: Immersive documentary day with Bajau community, editing session.
- Day 10: Fort Wolio, local market, traditional harbour, sunset.
- Day 11: Flexible exploration day based on weather and cultural opportunities, farewell dinner.
- Day 12: Departure from Baubau Airport.
